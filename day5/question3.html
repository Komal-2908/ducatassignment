Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  1. 1 and 1/2 cups of flour
  2. 1 cup of sugar
  3. 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  4. 1 and 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder
  5. 1 and 1/2 teaspoons of baking soda
  6. 1 teaspoon of salt
  7. 2 large eggs
  8. 1 cup of whole milk
  9. 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  10. 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  11. 1 cup of boiling water

Cooking Steps

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  • In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  • In another bowl, beat the eggs, then add the milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract, and mix well.
  • Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until well combined.
  • Stir in the boiling water until the batter is smooth. The batter will be thin.
  • Pour the batter evenly into the prepared baking pans.
  • Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  • Remove from the oven and allow the cakes to cool in the pans for about 10 minutes, then remove from the pans and cool completely on a wire rack.
  • Once cooled, frost with your favorite frosting and serve.
