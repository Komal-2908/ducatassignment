Overview:

World War II was a global war that lasted from 1939 to 1945, involving most of the world's nations. It was the most widespread war in history and directly involved more than 100 million people from over 30 countries.

Key Events:

  • Invasion of Poland
  • Battle of Britain
  • Pearl Harbor
  • D-Day
