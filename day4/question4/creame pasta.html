Creamy Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta (penne or fettuccine)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 50g grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and chopped onion, and saut√© until the onion is translucent. Add the heavy cream to the skillet and bring to a gentle simmer. Stir in the grated parmesan cheese until melted and well combined. Add the dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper to the cream sauce. Stir to mix well. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat with the creamy sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the pasta to absorb some of the sauce. Serve the creamy pasta hot, garnished with fresh parsley.
